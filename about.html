Gilbert Peralta is an associate professor of the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science (DMCS) at the University of the Philippines Baguio. He obtained his Doctor rerum naturalium (Dr.rer.nat.) at the Institute of Mathematics and Scientific Computing (IMSC), University of Graz in 2014 (under the supervision of Georg Propst). He was a former associate member of The International Research Training Group (IGDK 1754) “Optimization and Numerical Analysis for Partial Differential Equations with Nonsmooth Structures.” He was also a postdoctoral research assistant at IMSC under a grant from SFB Research Center Mathematical Optimization and Applications in Biomedical Sciences (MOBIS).
